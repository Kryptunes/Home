# Research Notes — AI Governance Gap

## Hypothesis

Enterprise AI pilots fail at the governance boundary — not at the model boundary. Organizations that define ownership before go-live have dramatically higher governance audit confidence.

## Key findings

| Metric | Pilot-stage orgs | Fully integrated orgs | Source |
|--------|------------------|----------------------|--------|
| Confident on 90-day governance audit | 7% | 74% | Grant Thornton 2026 |
| Pilots reaching production | ~12% | — | McKinsey/CIO 2026 |
